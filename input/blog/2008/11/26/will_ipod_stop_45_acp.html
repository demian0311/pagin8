iPod after Shooting
Originally uploaded by neidetcher
The in-laws were in town this week so I took them up to the range. We have an old iPod around that is fried so I thought it'd be a fun thing to shoot at.

Sure there's good training value to be had by shooting at paper targets but once in a while it's fun to take the boom stick to something else. Electronics are always a good choice.

Besides the damage pictured the battery was particularly hot. I assume some magic contents were touching that weren't meant to touch and the battery was discharging or something.

Indeed a .45 ACP round fired from my Kimber Custom 1911A1 clone goes right through an iPod. Good times.
